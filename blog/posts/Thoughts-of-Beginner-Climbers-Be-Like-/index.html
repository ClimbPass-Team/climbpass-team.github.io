Thoughts of Beginner Climbers Be Like…

Looks easy…I mean, the entire wall’s filled with rocks. Just grab any and climb up…or not. You mean we have to climb using only the same coloured rocks?!

That is freaking scary. Why would anyone subject themselves to the risk of fracturing bones as a hobby?

Man, that form and biceps. *inner swoon* Hats off to you, lady.

The bouldering wall doesn’t look that high. *Minutes later, three feet above ground* I am seriously contemplating life choices right now.

This harness makes me look like an overgrown baby.

Is this a red climbing hold, or a pink one? Too much chalk. (Surprise, it’s green! Just kidding.)

I can’t see where my next coloured hold is!!





*looks over to see children climbing* Man, that kid is fearless. And fast. And fierce. Those kids are amazing.

Ouch, just pulled a muscle. Should have done warm up but I didn’t, HAHA.

I do believe I’m looking very cool right now. That’s right, imma risk taker.

Hope I don’t look like an idiotic grasshopper. Aarrgghh who cares.

One, two, three, jump! *freezes* You were supposed to let go, traitorous fingers!

One, two, three, jump! *lands on bottom* The heck, Bruh? I thought you said you gonna catch me?





One, two, three, jump! *lands, stumbles, blinks eyes* Whoa, okay, that didn’t hurt as much as I expected. #YOLO #Let’s definitely try falling a second time!

So I searched online this morning that this is the best position to look awesome *twists neck to look over shoulder at the phone-camera friend is holding and giving a wide enough smile without pulling a tendon*

THESE SHOES. ARE. KILLING. ME. —TOE CRUSHERS!

Me, tired. Arms, sore. Hotel, Trivago.

Did you see that? DID YOU SEE THAT? Proudly defying gravity for one second. *referring to brief moment both hands were on final climbing hold before diving down ungracefully like a duck*

Beta…I need a beta…Time to copy some moves.

Okay, I’ve seen how, but thaaaat is impossible for me.

What’s a miracle, you say? Me climbing out of bed tomorrow.

Hmmm, what should I eat later…





This is…actually not bad.

*looks at equally lousy friend, jerks chin up* I challenge…you.

*notices equally lousy staring at you, nods* Challenge accepted.

(Afterwards)

HAHAHAHH I WON. HOW ‘BOUT THAT? CHAMPION OF THE CENTURY.

SHOOT, I CAN’T BELIEVE I LOST. THIS IS THE HUMILIATION OF A HUNDRED YEARS.

*As the third equally lousy friend, shakes head* Life is not a race. Rock climbing is not a race. But that was some pretty amusing entertainment.

I know this sounds dumb but it’s the sixth time I’m attempting this route and I’m stuck at the exact same spot in the exact same position. RIP.





Time to leave? Time flies too fast, honestly.

Not sure if I’m coming again. But today was fun. Perhaps I may ask my friends/family to join me next time.


Will you? :)
