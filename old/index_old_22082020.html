HOW IT WORKS

4 simple steps

Step 1: Choose your gym
Head to top of page, and click on 'Book with ClimbPass'. You should be redirected to a Google form where you can indicate which gym you want to climb at, and at your preferred timings.
Step 2: Online Payment
Make payment via PayNow at the designated number on the Google form.
Step 3: Email Confirmation
Your payment will be processed within 24 hours after you've paid, and you will receive an email confirmation from ClimbPass.
Step 4: Start climbing
On the day of your climb, show the email confirmation to the gym. The gym will authenticate you based on your unique code. You are good to climb!

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Some common questions

1. Who is ClimbPass for?

ClimbPass is for everyone. Whether you're a complete beginner to climbing, or have done it a few times, ClimbPass is for you!

2. Is equipment provided?

No, equipment is not included when you buy with ClimbPass. Climbing shoes typically costs 4-6 bucks for rental. If you want to try the high wall, you also have to rent the harness and belay device. This cost about 4-6 bucks on average.

3. Can I wear non-climbing shoes to climb?

No, only climbing shoes are allowed in climbing gyms. However, there are shoe rentals at every gym; do remember to bring socks for hygiene purposes.

4. Is climbing safe?

Climbing is a sport with some risks, but they can be mitigated with safety precautions and good practices. Safety mats are also present to cushion your fall when bouldering.

5. I can't do pull ups, how can I even climb?

In climbing, the routes are graded upon difficulty, from V0 to V8 in ascending order of difficulty. V0 routes are extremely beginner friendly to begin with, almost as easy as climbing a ladder!

6. Should I boulder or top rope first?

Every person is different, some people enjoy the exhilaration of climbing high and opt for the top rope option. If you are afraid of heights, bouldering might be a good way to start.
