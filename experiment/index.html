HOW IT WORKS

4 simple steps

Creating ‘Jio’ has never been easier

Detailed customization

Create and customize your jio in less than 30 seconds. Specify the gym, date, time and if you have multipasses to share. See how many available slots are there for people to join the jio. Bookmark a jio if in advance if you are planning to climb but are unsure of your schedule. Share a jio with a friend if you and your friend wants to climb together with a more experienced climber

Joining a jio has never been simpler

Detailed customization

Feeling spontaneous? See who’s climbing today and might have passes to share Need a belayer? See who’s looking for a top rope partner Want to improve your climbing skills? Match with a climber with similar experience as you. Sharing is caring. Feel free to request a multipass from someone who is willing to share.

1-click matching

Detailed customization

Scheduling back and forth is too troublesome. Found a jio you like? Just click 1 button to join the jio. Once your request has been accepted by the host, you will be invited to a group chat. Everyone hates unresponsive members, so we implemented a review system that penalizes people who go missing.
