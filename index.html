OUR STORY

How It All Started

Summitting Breakneck Ridge in Cold Spring, New York, Ernest had a taste of what rock climbing was (literally), being on all fours, and trying to climb over rocks. He fell in love with it, and started to pursue indoor climbing at gyms such as Brooklyn Boulders and Steep Rock.

Upon returning to Singapore, Ernest realised that climbing was an expensive sport. The cheapest way to climb, was to get a season pass, but that meant that he was stuck to one gym, as paying for a day pass elsewhere was costly.

Hence, ClimbPass was born, a climbing platform to allow climbers to access more gyms at the same price, and also introduce climbing to non-climbers by allowing them to match with more experienced climbers.
