OUR STORY

How It All Started

Summitting Breakneck Ridge in Cold Spring, New York, Ernest had a taste of what rock climbing was (literally). He fell in love with it, and started to pursue indoor climbing at gyms such as Brooklyn Boulders and Steep Rock in New York City.

Returning to Singapore, Ernest realised that the most affordable way to climb, was to get a season pass. However, that meant that he was stuck to one gym, as paying for a day pass elsewhere was too costly.

Hence, ClimbPass was born, a climbing platform to allow climbers to access more gyms at more affordable prices, and also introduce climbing to non-climbers by allowing them to match with more experienced climbers.
